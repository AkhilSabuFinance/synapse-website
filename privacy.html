Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Overview

This policy explains how Synapse handles information collected through this website and waitlist. The current site is designed to collect only the information needed to manage interest, share updates, and prepare early access.

Information We Collect

If you join the waitlist, we collect your email address. We may also receive basic technical information such as time of submission, browser information, IP address, referring page, and site interaction data from hosting, database, analytics, or security providers.

Health Data

The current website waitlist does not ask you to submit health, medical, genetic, biometric, wearable, lab, or clinical information. Do not submit sensitive health information through the waitlist form or community channels.

How We Use Information

We use waitlist information to confirm submissions, send Synapse updates, manage early access, understand launch interest, improve the website, protect the service from abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

Legal Basis

Where applicable, we process information based on your consent, our legitimate interest in operating and improving Synapse, the need to perform requested services, and compliance with legal obligations.

Sharing

We do not sell waitlist emails. We may share information with trusted service providers that help us host the website, store waitlist submissions, send updates, secure the service, or understand basic site performance.

Third Party Services

The site may use third party services such as hosting providers, database providers, email tools, X, Telegram, EasyA, DexScreener, and token pages. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

Retention

We keep waitlist information for as long as needed to operate the waitlist, send relevant updates, manage early access, resolve disputes, maintain security, and meet legal requirements. You can request removal at any time.

Your Choices

You can ask to access, correct, delete, or stop receiving updates related to your waitlist information. You can also unsubscribe from marketing updates where an unsubscribe option is provided.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information submitted through the site. No internet service is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children

The site and waitlist are not intended for children under 13, or a higher age where required by local law. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

International Use

If you access the site from outside the location where our providers operate, your information may be processed in other countries with different data protection laws.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as Synapse develops. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

For privacy questions or removal requests, contact Synapse through X or Telegram.
